About Me

Hi there! I’m Abdelmoudjib. If you want to know a bit more about me, read on!

My passion is using engineering to improve people’s lives, and giving people the tools they need to do the same.

Currently, I’m the PhD student at VEDECOM institute, where we’re developing the car of the future. My PhD project is a collaboration between VEDECOM, ESTACA engineering school and Lorraine university.

I received my Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Industrial Computing from Lorraine university, Metz, France. I received also an engineering degree from the National Polytechnic School, Oran, Algeria.

Research Interest

My research work is mainly in the field of artificial intelligence for different applications of autonomous driving. The main objective of my PhD thesis is to estimate road user intentions for the autonomous vehicle.
